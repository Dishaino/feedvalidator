2006-04-15: libraries for the feed validator's API

In addition to the existing WebService::Validator::Feed::W3C for perl, a new FeedValidator library, for Ruby and Rails is now available to access the W3C Feed validation service through its SOAP API.

2005-11-21: Launch of the W3C Feed Validation Service

Launch of the W3C Feed Validation Service, a free syntax checker for feeds in formats such as RSS and Atom. Building upon the open source software feedvalidator, this service also acts as a Web Service with an open API.

These are the most recent news for this service. News for the feedvalidator software are also available at feedvalidator.org.
